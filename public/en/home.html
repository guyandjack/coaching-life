What is coaching?

See the services

Coaching is personalized support that helps individuals and groups achieve personal and professional and professional goals.

It is based on a relationship of trust between coach and client, using various techniques to stimulate reflection and maximize potential.

The coach explores, actively listens, questions, understands the deepest needs for transform them into SMART objectives (Specific, Measurable, Appropriate, Realistic, Timely) objectives, encourages and gives feedback.

Deontology and ethics

See the services

The deontology and ethics of coaching according to ICF(International Coaching Federation) are based on fundamental principles.

This includes respecting confidentiality, integrity, authenticity, and the client's right to determine their own goals. The coach is co-responsible for creating a space of trust and safety for the coachee.

ICF coaches commit to maintaining high standards of professionalism and competence while respecting cultural and individual diversity.

How does a coaching session unfold?

See the services

After the introductory session, we move on to a coaching session. This begins with setting clear and specific goals for the session.

The coach actively listens, asks questions, and uses various coaching tools to stimulate the client’s reflection. Coaching techniques are used to explore challenges, identify resources, and develop a concrete action plan.

Each coaching session ends with concrete commitments and tasks to be completed for the next meeting. These are always defined in co-construction between the coach and the client.

What is the interaction between the coach and the client?

See the services

The collaboration between the coach and the client: It is a collaboration based on trust, respect, and confidentiality.

The coach acts as a neutral and supportive partner, offering non-judgmental support to help the coachee achieve their goals.

This collaboration is client-centered, promoting the empowerment and accountability of the coachee in their journey towards change and growth.

What is efficient coaching?

See the services

The effectiveness of personalized coaching can be measured in various ways.

This can include the evaluation of the progress made by the coachee toward achieving their goals, the coachee's satisfaction with the process and results, as well as the observable changes in the coachee's behaviors, skills, or perspectives.

Regular feedback and check-ins at different stages of the coaching process also help measure effectiveness and adjust approaches if necessary.
