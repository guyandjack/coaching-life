Who am I?

As a passionate Human Resources Director, my professional journey has allowed me to evolve in various contexts, both in Switzerland and internationally. Today, I offer my knowledge and experience to support you in your professional and personal development.

Values I identify with

  • "Enthusiasm"

Empathy, authenticity, and collaboration are the pillars of my approach. I firmly believe that each individual is unique and deserves a personalized approach to achieve their goals. My commitment to mental and professional well-being guides all my actions. My goal is to create an environment conducive to your personal and professional growth.
I am also a member of the Genuine Women community, an active and dynamic network of women entrepreneurs, both in Switzerland and worldwide. Sharing common values, we regularly exchange and support each other in developing our projects.
